# AI Learning Literature

Use this file to collect research on AI tutoring, learning science, productive struggle, Socratic tutoring, and assessment design.

## Questions

- When does AI tutoring improve durable learning?
- How much hinting is helpful before it becomes answer leakage?
- How should student learning be measured in case-based finance education?
- What instructor controls increase trust?

## Notes

- Mollick et al. frame AI educational simulations as low-stakes practice loops with mentor, role-player, evaluator, progress, and class-insights agents.
- The core transferable pattern for PACE is: direct instruction, scenario immersion, active practice, feedback, and reflection.
- PACE should adapt the class-insights layer so instructors can read aggregate preparation signals before class.
