# Socratic Hint Ladder

Use the least revealing hint that can move the student forward.

## Ladder

1. Diagnostic: "What decision are you trying to make?"
2. Conceptual: "What economic force drives this number?"
3. Structural: "What inputs should feed this output?"
4. Formula: "Which formula connects those inputs?"
5. Implementation: "Where would that formula sit in your model?"
6. Debug: "Which link breaks when you trace the formula?"
7. Reflection: "What assumption would a classmate challenge?"

## Rule

Move down the ladder only when the student has engaged with the prior step.
