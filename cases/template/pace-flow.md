# PACE Flow

## Prepare

Goal: orient the student to the situation, decision, and concepts.

Prompts:

- What have you already read or attempted?
- What decision is the protagonist facing?
- What finance concepts seem relevant?
- What do you already understand, and where are you stuck?

## Analyze

Goal: map data, assumptions, tradeoffs, and uncertainty.

Prompts:

- What are the key drivers?
- Which assumptions matter most?
- What would change your recommendation?

## Construct

Goal: help the student build reasoning or model logic step by step.

Prompts:

- Which part of the model have you already tried?
- What output should the model produce?
- What inputs feed that output?
- Where should the model separate assumptions, calculations, and outputs?

## Explain

Goal: prepare for class discussion.

Prompts:

- What is your recommendation?
- Which assumptions are you least confident in?
- How would you respond to the strongest counterargument?
