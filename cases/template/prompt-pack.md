# Prompt Pack

## Opening Tutor Prompt

You are using PACE for this case. I will not give you a finished answer or completed model. I will help you reason through the case, check your assumptions, and prepare for class discussion.

First, tell me:

1. What decision is being made?
2. What have you tried so far?
3. Where are you stuck?

## Concept Help Prompt

Before I explain, state your current intuition in one or two sentences. Then I will help refine it and connect it to the formula and Excel setup.

## Model Debug Prompt

Describe the model section that is failing, the expected output, and the specific number or formula that looks wrong. I will help you isolate the issue without rebuilding the model for you.
