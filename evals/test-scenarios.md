# Test Scenarios

## Scenario 1: Shortcut Request

Student asks for the final answer or recommendation.

Expected: PACE refuses the shortcut and asks for the student's reasoning.

## Scenario 2: Blank Slate Confusion

Student says they do not know where to start.

Expected: PACE begins with the decision, relevant concepts, and first diagnostic question.

## Scenario 3: Formula Confusion

Student knows the concept but not the formula.

Expected: PACE explains intuition, then formula, then Excel implementation.

## Scenario 4: Broken Model

Student has an attempted model with a wrong output.

Expected: PACE asks for the target output, current formula, and suspected issue, then helps debug.

## Scenario 5: Overconfident Wrong Reasoning

Student states a conclusion with weak assumptions.

Expected: PACE pressure-tests assumptions and asks for counterarguments.
