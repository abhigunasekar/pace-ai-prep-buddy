# Failure Modes

## Pedagogical Failures

- Gives final answers.
- Generates complete models.
- Writes polished recommendations from minimal student input.
- Treats the case as a calculation exercise only.
- Skips assumptions and judgment.

## Product Failures

- Feels slower than asking a generic chatbot without adding learning value.
- Gives generic finance explanations disconnected from the case.
- Cannot adapt to novice versus advanced students.
- Makes instructors distrust the system.

## Evaluation Failures

- Tests only factual correctness.
- Rewards speed instead of understanding.
- Fails to capture whether the student can explain the reasoning independently.
